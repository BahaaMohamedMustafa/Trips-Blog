Have you ever thought or dreamed about swimming with an apex predator? If you love sharks
this is a fin-tastic experience, you will be able to safely experience the awe and beauty of sharks
in the open ocean. Our expert team will keep tabs on you and ensure that you will be
completely safe.
The warm waters of the Caribbean are home to all kinds of sharks, this trip will allow you to see
many different species and the focus of this experience is to educate our guests and help to
dispel some of the common stereotypes that many people have when they think of sharks. The
waters off the Bahamas are known for nurse sharks, lemon sharks, reef sharks, tiger sharks, as
well as the iconic hammerhead shark, depending on the season. And when it comes to getting
close to these giant beauties, it pays to book a trip with an outfitter that knows what they\u2019re
doing \u2013 that would be us!
Did you know more people die from taking selfies every year compared to shark attacks? What
about driving in a car? flying on a plane? Getting a soda from a vending machine? All of these
activities have a higher probability of being injured and\/or killed when compared to shark
attacks.
In an effort to educate people on the true behavior of sharks \u2014 often calm and curious, not
aggressive \u2014 we offer educational experiences that allow people to see firsthand what
swimming with them is really like. Our hope is that these experiences may help to clear up any
misconceptions and inspire people to advocate for these amazing creatures.
Consider joining us for this incredible experience; what makes this trip special is the sheer
quantity of sharks; you can dive with as many as 50 at a time \u2014 a prospect that is at once both
intimidating and awe-inspiring. We will also see lots of other beautiful sea creatures and
pristine reefs .<\/p>\n\n\n\n
