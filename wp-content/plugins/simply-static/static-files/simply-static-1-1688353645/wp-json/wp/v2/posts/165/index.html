Palau is a small country located in pacific Micronesia, it is made up of over 300 islands. It is one
of the top marine tourist destinations and the cool part is the island is a carbon-neutral tourism
destination. The waters of Palau boast an abundance of marine life with well over 500 coral
species and thousands of diverse fish types. One of the most widely known gems of Palau is the
jellyfish lake where you can safely swim with thousands of stingless jellyfish. If you love the
ocean Palau is a place you will not want to miss.
